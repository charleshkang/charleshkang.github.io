RideTracker

RideTracker is a simple app focusing on NSUserDefaults. It's an app that allows users to keep track of how many times they've taken the subway, and tracks their metrocard balance accordingly. I built it because I was using the notes app to keep track, but I wanted to build something simple to do it for me. It's mainly for those who do not use unlimited metrocards.
