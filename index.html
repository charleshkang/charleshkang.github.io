My Work

Vimo is a visually appealing music app using the Spotify iOS SDK. It is gesture-driven to allow quick access in and out of your music player.

Neverlate is an elegant location-based self-improvement app that makes sure you're on time by making you pay your friend if you're late!

About

I'm a California-native but grew up there and in South Korea. I'm a professionally trained chef turned iOS Developer. I heard about the Access Code program and applied, graduating from a 9-month intensive development program.

Since then, I've worked on several of my own apps and submitted 2 of them to the app store. I love creating things from nothing. What I love about cooking and iOS development is I can take an idea, and with great execution and thought, produce an awesome product that many people love. I enjoy building my apps in a wholistic manner, which includes both designing and developing the product.
